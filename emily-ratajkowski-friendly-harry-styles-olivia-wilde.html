Emily Ratajkowski Has Been &quot;Friendly&quot; with Harry Styles and Olivia Wilde

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, May 31, 2024

Though the video of Harry Styles and Emily Ratajkowski making out in Tokyo shocked fans when it went viral on the Internet this past weekend, maybe it shouldn't have.

According to a source for People, the two have long been acquainted with each other, even before locking lips: "Harry and Emily know each other," the insider told the outlet. "They have been friendly for a while."

The source added that the model is also "friendly" with Styles's ex Olivia Wilde, whom he dated from late 2020 to November 2022.

In a video of Styles and Ratajkowski published by the Daily Mail, the two can be seen kissing while leaning against a van in Tokyo, where the "Watermelon Sugar" singer recently performed during his worldwide Love on Tour concert series.

Valentina Frugiuele//Getty Images

Axelle/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

Last year, Ratajkowski made the decision to split from her then husband, Sebastian Bear-McClard, with whom she shares two-year-old son Sylvester Apollo.

The High Low podcast host opened up about being single for the first time in her adult life for Harper's BAZAAR's November 2022 cover story.

"It was hard for me to go on a date with someone and think about how much I did or didn't like them. I would have been thinking about how they were perceiving me, what it meant, what they wanted from me, what it meant about my self-worth," she said. "I don't have that anymore. So now it's really fun to go to dinner with someone and be like, 'Cool. I really enjoyed these parts of them. I really didn't like these other parts.'"
