Song Hye-Kyo Boyfriend: Who Is She Dating Now? Kids And Relationship Timeline With Song Joong-ki

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, February 17, 2024

Song Hye-Kyo boyfriend is a hot topic worldwide. While her former husband has confirmed redating, is she seeing anyone?

The South Korean Actress rose to international fame through her leading roles in the television dramas Full House, Autumn in My Heart, All In, and The Wind Blows.

Her other famous projects include That Winter, Descendants of the Sun, Encounter, Now, and We Are Breaking Up, among others.

Likewise, Hye-Kyo has starred in films such as Hwang Jin Yi, The Grandmaster, My Brilliant Life, and The Queens, among others.

The South Korean celeb ranked 6th in Forbes magazine’s Korea Power Celebrity list in 2018.

Song Hye-Kyo Boyfriend: Who Is She Dating Now? Does She Have Kids?

Song Hye-Kyo is believed to be currently single. She has not been caught in any relationship rumors since her divorce from ex-husband Song Joong Ki.

Instead, she appears to focus on her growing career and beautiful time with friends.

On her 41st birthday photo in November 2022, the Korean star wrote that she enjoys her solitude and unchanging love and support from her fans.

Who’s Dated Who reports that she has previously been in relationships with Hyun Bin (2010-2011), Lee Byung-hun (2003-2004), and Seong-Su Kim.

Besides, Hye-Kyo was married for two years to Actor Song Joong-ki (2017-2019). They did not have any children; however, Hye-Kyo once mentioned she would love to have three kids.

Song Hye-Kyo Relationship Timeline With Song Joong-ki

Song Hye-Kyo and her Descendants of the Sun co-star Song Joong-ki announced their engagement through their respective agencies on July 5, 2017.

They subsequently married in a private ceremony at Youngbingwan, Hotel Shilla in Seoul, on October 31, 2017. 

The pair’s closest family and friends attended the wedding ceremony, including actors Yoo Ah-in, Lee Kwang-soo, and Park Bo-gum, who also played the piano at the reception.

The event became the talk of the town with intense media interest across Asia.

Unfortunately, things didn’t work out. On June 27, 2019, Joong-ki revealed that he had filed for divorce from Hye-Kyo the previous day.

The divorce was finalized in July of that year. The marriage of the power couple briefly lasted for nearly two years.

Is Song Joong Ki Dating Katy Louise Saunders?

It appears that Song Joong Ki has found love again. Following online reports of the Vincenzo Actor dating, his agency HighZium Studio has confirmed his relationship.

According to his management, who confirmed Joong Ki’s relationship status with Soompi, “Actor Song Joong Ki is now dating a woman with great feelings. We hope you will find their bond endearing.

Additionally, the agency asked that fans appreciate that they can only confirm their relationship and nothing else.

The Company said, “We would appreciate it if you refrain from posting any speculative or unsubstantiated reports.”

The Actor is reportedly going out with a British non-celebrity woman.

While it is claimed that his girlfriend is not a celebrity, netizens speculated that Joong Ki is dating British Actress Katy Louise Saunders.

Although there is no confirmation, their photos and videos have gone viral on the internet.

It is believed that Katy met Song through a mutual friend in 2021 and accompanied him for the international promotions of his show Reborn Rich.

Following the recent dating rumors, Joong Ki’s wedding photos with his ex-wife Song Hye-Kyo also circulated on social media.

Joong Ki is now in his first committed relationship after his breakup with Hye-Kyo. The divorce of the co-stars of Descendants of the Sun came as a huge shock to viewers worldwide.

Song Joon Ki is one of the most popular Actors in South Korea. He made his debut with A Frozen Flower and recently starred in K-drama Reborn Rich.
