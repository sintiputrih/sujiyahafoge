Can you paint FRP wall board?

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

Answer

When painting fibreglass reinforced plastic, it is important to use the right paint. Most fibreglass reinforced plastic (FRP) textured wall panels are not intended to be painted, and this is a general rule. Instead, they are manufactured with built-in colour that has been applied at the factory, which helps to reduce the effect of scratches and other surface damage on the final product.

 

Is it possible to paint fibreglass reinforced plastic (FRP) board in this situation?

Painting FRP is not suggested since the finish is intended to be a non-adhesive surface and so cannot be painted. If painting is required, epoxy or acrylic-based paints are recommended..
