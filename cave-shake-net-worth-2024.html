The Founding of Cave Shake

Cave Shake was founded in 2013 by Holly Heath and Billie Cavallaro, who identified a gap in the market for a product that could satisfy the cravings of those following a ketogenic diet without breaking their nutritional goals.

The founders’ personal experiences with the keto lifestyle fueled their passion for creating a product that was both indulgent and compliant with dietary restrictions.
