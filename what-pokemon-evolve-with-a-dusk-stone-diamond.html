What Pokemon evolve with a Dusk Stone diamond?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, September 14, 2024

1. Murkrow

To evolve Murkrow into Honchkrow in Pokemon Brilliant Diamond, you can use a Dusk Stone.

2. Misdreavus

To evolve Misdreavus into Mismagius in Pokemon Shining Pearl, you can use a Dusk Stone.

3. Mismagius

Mismagius evolves from Misdreavus when exposed to a Dusk Stone.

4. Honchkrow

Honchkrow evolves from Murkrow when exposed to a Dusk Stone.

5. Doublade

Doublade does not evolve with a Dusk Stone. It evolves into Aegislash when exposed to a Dusk Stone.

6. Aegislash

Aegislash evolves from Doublade when exposed to a Dusk Stone.

7. Lampent

Lampent evolves into Chandelure when exposed to a Dusk Stone.

8. Chandelure

Chandelure evolves from Lampent when exposed to a Dusk Stone.

9. Dusknoir

Dusknoir does not evolve with a Dusk Stone. It evolves from Dusclops when traded holding a Reaper Cloth.

10. Dusclops

Dusclops evolves into Dusknoir when traded while holding a Reaper Cloth.

11. Runerigus

Runerigus does not evolve with a Dusk Stone. It evolves from Galarian Yamask when leveled up in a specific location.

12. Yamask

Yamask evolves into Runerigus when leveled up in a specific location.

13. Gigalith

Gigalith does not evolve with a Dusk Stone. It evolves from Boldore when traded.

14. Boldore

Boldore evolves into Gigalith when traded.

15. Misdreavus

Misdreavus evolves into Mismagius when exposed to a Dusk Stone in Pokemon Shining Pearl.

16. Spritzee

Spritzee does not evolve with a Dusk Stone. It evolves into Aromatisse when traded while holding a Sachet.

17. Aromatisse

Aromatisse evolves from Spritzee when traded while holding a Sachet.

18. Roselia

Roselia can evolve into Roserade when exposed to a Shiny Stone.

19. Togetic

Togetic can evolve into Togekiss when exposed to a Shiny Stone.

20. Togekiss

Togekiss evolves from Togetic when exposed to a Shiny Stone.

21. Floette

Floette does not evolve with a Dusk Stone. It evolves into Florges with the use of a Shiny Stone.

22. Florges

Florges evolves from Floette when exposed to a Shiny Stone.
