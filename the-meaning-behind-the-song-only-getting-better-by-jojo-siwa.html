The Meaning Behind The Song: Only Getting Better by JoJo Siwa

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, September 13, 2024

As a Music Therapist, I have had the privilege of witnessing the profound impact that music can have on individuals. I have seen how certain songs can uplift spirits, provide comfort, and offer a sense of hope. One such song that has resonated with many of my clients is “Only Getting Better” by JoJo Siwa.

I first heard this song on a rainy afternoon while scrolling through my playlist, searching for a tune to brighten my mood. Little did I know that I would stumble upon a song that would not only lift my spirits but also inspire me to share its message with others.

“Only Getting Better” is an upbeat, country-tinged pop song about always hoping for the best, even when it seems like the world is upside down. JoJo Siwa, known for her vibrant personality and infectious energy, brings her signature enthusiasm to this track. She has often described it as her “first ever ballad,” and her emotional investment in the song is evident in her powerful vocals.

The lyrics of the song speak of resilience, optimism, and a determination to keep moving forward. It reminds us that life is full of twists and turns, but we have the strength to navigate through them. Lines like “Hangin’ on for another curve” and “Take a chance, it’ll all be easier in time” emphasize the importance of embracing change and embracing the unknown.

Listening to “Only Getting Better” fills me with a sense of hope and possibility. It reminds me that no matter what challenges we face, there is always a light at the end of the tunnel. The song encourages us to keep pushing forward, to take risks, and to believe that things will indeed get better.

I have played this song for many of my clients, and the response has been overwhelmingly positive. It has helped individuals facing difficult life circumstances to find solace and strength. There is something magical about the power of music to touch our hearts and provide comfort when we need it the most.

The music video for “Only Getting Better” showcases JoJo Siwa’s vibrant personality through colorful visuals and energetic choreography. It perfectly compliments the uplifting message of the song and adds an extra layer of joy and excitement.

In conclusion, “Only Getting Better” by JoJo Siwa is not just a catchy pop song, but a reminder to never lose hope. It serves as a powerful anthem for anyone facing adversity or uncertainty, reminding us that as long as we stick together, things will always get better. So, the next time you find yourself in need of some motivation, give this song a listen and let its uplifting energy fill your heart.
