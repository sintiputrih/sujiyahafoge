Need something to remember the good memories with? This guide will teach you all there is to know about them.

The Purpose of a Locket 

A locket is a small, wearer case that can hold mementos, pictures, or other significant symbols or text. Lockets are used to easily remember things that you want to cherish, and they also serve as a romantic gift.

Lockets can come in a variety of shapes and sizes, with one of the most common being the heart shape. Other shapes also exist, but you will generally find lockets with pictures in a heart shape.

Why You Should Get a Locket With Pictures

They help you remember and cherish memories

Even if you aren’t that old, a locket can really help you remember the past. Many people are born with imperfect memory, easily forgetting things that others would otherwise remember without issue. 

With the help of a locket, remembering the past is as simple as opening the locket’s clasp. The sentimental aspect of the memory will make it more vivid as well, helping you retain that memory for years and even decades to come.

They’re symbolic

Have you ever wondered why lockets were often shaped after a heart? When you wear a locket around your neck, it rests just above the area in front of your heart. So in a literal and figurative sense, the memory inside the locket is close to your heart. 

This symbolism carries a lot of meaning for some people.

What Are Lockets With Pictures Sourced From? 

Gold

Gold is one of, if not the most, popular precious metals, and it’s no secret that it’s used in many pieces of jewelry. You can get lockets either gold-plated, as a gold alloy, or made out of pure gold. Since pure gold is highly malleable, it’s far better to get either a gold alloy or a gold-plated locket for everyday use.

Silver

Aside from gold, silver is another popular precious metal. In jewelry-making, sterling silver is often blended with pure silver to strengthen the necklace and make it better suited for daily use.

The Average Price of a Locket With Pictures 

You’d be surprised by how affordable lockets can be, even the custom-made ones. For less than $20 you can get a locket of decent quality with customized text. This may either be printed or engraved. 

At higher price points you may also get a free photo of your choice, as well as package deals such as two lockets for couples. If you want a locket made out of pure precious metals and gems, however, then you can expect to look at a higher price point depending on the metals and stones used.
