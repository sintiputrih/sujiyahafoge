Biography

Tyler Summitt was born in Tennessee on September 21, 1990. College basketball coach who became the head coach of the Louisiana Tech University women’s basketball team in 2014. He previously served as an assistant coach at Marquette and the University of Tennessee. He took over for Teresa Weatherspoon as the head coach of the Louisiana Tech University Lady Techsters. He played high school basketball at the Webb School of Knoxville and was later a guard for the Tennessee Volunteers.
