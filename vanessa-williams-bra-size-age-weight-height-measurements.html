Biography:

Vanessa Williams was born on March 18, 1963 in Millwood, New York, U.S. She first came to prominence when she became the first African-American to be crowned Miss America. She, however, relinquished her crown when Penthouse magazine published nude photographs of her.

She moved on by launching a successful career in the entertainment industry, receiving numerous Grammy, Emmy and Tony Award nominations. She is considered the most successful Miss America winner in the entertainment industry.

Her debut album, The Right Stuff, was released in 1988 and produced the hit songs “The Right Stuff” and “Dreamin'”.
