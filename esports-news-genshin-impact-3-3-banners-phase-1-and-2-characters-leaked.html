Genshin Impact 3.3 banners: Phase 1 and 2 characters leaked

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, October 4, 2024

With the ongoing update of Genshin Impact nearing its second phase, players are impatiently waiting to know the characters that will be accompanying the new releases, Scaramouche and Faruzan in the next patch. Fortunately, a reliable leaker has been able to determine the upcoming banners through in-game files.

Genshin Impact 3.3 banners will be available in the game from December and continue till the next year. As per the Chinese leaker who goes by the name ‘Uncle Lu’, new banners will be released in the following order-

First phase

Scaramouche + Itto (Faruzan as the 4-star character)

Second phase

Raiden Shogun + (unknown)

There is a possibility of a third rerun banner in the 3.3 update which could either be of Kamisato Ayato or Eula. Out of these two characters, Ayato’s probability seems higher as he will be an important character for the Spiral Abyss buffs. On the other hand, Eula has not got a rerun banner since the 2.3 update.

Genshin Impact 3.3 banner details

The ongoing banner of Nahida and Yoimiya will soon be replaced by Yae Miko and Childe in the second phase of the 3.2 update. Following which, players can see the release of Scaramouche, another Fatui Harbinger who will be known as ‘The Wanderer’ when he becomes playable.

As mentioned by the leaker, Wanderer will be released right in the first phase which confirms his release date of be 7th December, as stated previously by HoYoverse when the updates were shortened. Scaramouche’s banner will be seen with Arataki Itto, a 5-star Geo character and their banner will comprise of Faruzan, the new 4-star character that is likely an Anemo support to Wanderer.

The next phase is confirmed to see the banner of Raiden Shogun, the Electro Archon, a character which will be quite crucial for the in-game lore and Archon Quest, considering that her puppet ‘Wanderer’ is becoming playable. However, it cannot be confirmed at this moment whether Genshin Impact will bring back Eula or Ayato in the second phase of 3.3 update.

For the weapon banner, it goes without saying that players can wish upon the signature weapon of Scaramouche and Itto as they will be the banners for the first phase. Here are their weapons-

  • Tullaytullah’s Remembrance (Wanderer’s Signature)
  • Redhorn Stonethresher

The second phase will bring the 5-star polearm ‘Engulfing Lightning’ as it is the signature weapon for Raiden Shogun. Along with that, the weapon for Ayato or Eula could also make a rerun if the characters return in Genshin Impact 3.3 update.
