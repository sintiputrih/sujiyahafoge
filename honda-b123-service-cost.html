Honda B123 Service Cost

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, February 20, 2024

Introduction

Owning a Honda is a matter of pride for many people around the world. The Japanese automaker is famous for making reliable and long-lasting vehicles. However, even the most reliable cars need to be serviced at regular intervals to keep them running smoothly. Honda has a set of services called the B123 service that is performed at specific times to ensure that the car stays in good condition. Let’s explore what the B123 service entails and its associated costs.

What is the B123 Service?

The B123 service is a set of scheduled maintenance procedures that Honda recommends for vehicles at specific intervals. The B123 service is performed when the odometer of the car reaches 15,000 miles, 45,000 miles, and 75,000 miles. The B123 service includes all the regular maintenance procedures such as oil change, tire rotation, and inspection of various parts of the car. However, it also includes some additional procedures that are not part of regular maintenance.

B123 Service Cost

The cost of the B123 service varies depending on the dealer and the location. However, the cost is generally between $200-$400. The cost may also depend on the model of the Honda vehicle you own. Some models may require more extensive procedures, which may increase the cost. However, it is always a good idea to check with your dealer to get an exact estimate of the cost before availing of the B123 service.

What Does the B123 Service Include?

The B123 service includes various maintenance procedures that are specific to the model and make of the Honda vehicle. Here is a detailed breakdown of what the B123 service includes:

B1 Service:

  • Engine oil and filter replacement
  • Tire rotation
  • Multi-point inspection

2 Service:

  • Engine air filter replacement
  • Cabin air filter replacement
  • Transmission fluid replacement
  • Rear differential fluid replacement (if applicable)

3 Service:

  • Spark plug replacement (applicable to certain models)
  • Inspection of various components such as the suspension and brake system
  • Replacement of the timing belt (if applicable)
