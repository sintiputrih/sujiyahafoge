David Riden Arrested What Did The Football Coach Do? Charges

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, September 30, 2024

David Riden was arrested on August 25 for the invasion of privacy charges at a high school. What exactly did he do?

David Riden is the assistant coach of the football team at Los Osos High School. He was charged with placing cameras in the girl’s bathroom at the school.

Why Was David Riden Arrested? Charges

David Riden was arrested for placing a covert recording device in the girls’ restroom at Los Osos High School. He has been charged with invasion of privacy.

The device was discovered by one of the staff members at the school and the investigation was initiated.

The police finally found appropriate evidence showing David place the camera in the room which was used by the students at the school.

The police searched the car and house of the accused and found further recording devices. David was then taken into custody.

The camera was detected a few days after he placed them based on the investigations made by the detectives. It is disgusting what he did considering how he was a part of the boy’s football team.

David Riden Was The Assistant Coach At The High School

David Riden had been working in the school as an assistant football coach to the varsity football team at the school since 2015. He was also the locker room attendant at the boy’s locker room.

Since the accusations, he has resigned from the position at the school and no longer works there.

The school put out a notice apologizing for the actions of the coach and has promised to do better to protect the integrity and privacy of all the students that go there.

Where Is He Now?

As the damning evidence was found against David, he was transported to the Rancho Cucamonga Police Department for further inquiries.

He was interrogated and by the end, he confessed to the charges placed on him. He was then arrested and assigned to the West Valley Detention Centre for the charges mentioned.

An investigation is undergoing to find out if any other members of the coaching team were involved in the incident.

The police will soon run a forensic run on the indicted items as a part of the investigation.

The detectives assured that this is an ongoing investigation and further information on the event will be released and updated throughout the day.
