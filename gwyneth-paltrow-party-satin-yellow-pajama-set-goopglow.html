Gwyneth Paltrow Celebrates Goopglow in a Satin Yellow Pajama Set

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, July 9, 2024

Gwyneth Paltrow partied the night away in East Hampton this week to celebrate the launch of Goopglow.

The actress-turned-wellness guru hosted a dreamy fete for her dearest and most influential friends yesterday to debut her latest skin- and haircare drop.

Bryan Bedder//Getty Images

The dress code seemed to be elevated sleepwear, because the host and her guests all gathered in their most luxurious pajamas. Paltrow wore a silky lemon-yellow set consisting of a button-up shirt, loose stretchy pants, and a structured bra top. She finished the sunny look with a pair of lime-green feathered slippers and a dainty gold chain necklace.

As per usual, the Emma star kept her beauty look natural, opting for a hydrated but makeup-free face, and a relaxed bun.

Bryan Bedder//Getty Images

Friends in attendance included Amy Griffin, Savannah Guthrie, Jo Ellison, and Derek Blasberg.

Bryan Bedder//Getty Images

The theme of the evening seemed to be a nature-inspired dreamland, as Paltrow partenered with Cartier to host the intimate dinner under a white pergola in a garden, decorated with white and purple flowers on the tables and ceiling, long candles in glass vases, and sprinkled fairy lights.

Bryan Bedder//Getty Images

The former actress was seen delivering a speech to her guests before kicking off the dinner. Next to her sat Guthrie, who wore a silky white pajama set with pink flowers and carried a dazzling bag, also in pink, for the event.

Bryan Bedder//Getty Images

Goopglow's new products include a multivitamin, the antioxidant-filled Morning Skin Superpowder, the Flower Acids toner, an exfoliating cleanser, a vitamin C serum, a dark spot exfoliating sleek milk, and a hair serum, among other things.
