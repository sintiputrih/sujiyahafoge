What Is Cameron Latu Nationality? Meet The NFL Player On Instagram

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, January 11, 2024

Junior footballer Cameron Latu is in the limelight for his spectacular victory over Miami Hurricanes. Read more to find out his nationality and ethnicity.

Cameron Latu is an emerging American footballer. He is currently a junior linebacker at the Alabama Crimson Tide. He rose to fame for his recent match against Miami.

Latu began playing football at Olympus High School. He was a top weakside defensive end in 2018. Moreover, he is a unanimous four-star prospect and the no.2 player in Utah.

While at his high school, Cameron played for The Titans and participated in Nike’s The Opening. In addition, he was chosen to compete for the Polynesian Bowl.

Upon graduation, Latu gained offers from the universities like Notre Dame, Oregon, and Nebraska. However, he committed to Alabama over any of them.

Cameron Latu Nationality & Ethnicity Revealed

Talking about nationality, Cameron Latu is an American citizen.

He grew up in Salt Lake City, Utah. As of now, he is a student at the University of Alabama and a player for their football team, Alabama Crimson Tide.

Further, Latu has Polynesian ethnicity as noted by Alabama Rivals.
