The MMA fighter was born to American parents on 11th April 1995 in Dearborn, Michigan, United States of America. His zodiac sign is Aries and currently resides in his home state. As of 2020, Josh Brueckner’s age is 25 years.

Inspired by mixed martial arts since a very young age, Brueckner turned his passion into a career. He has been very close with the Michigan Top Team gym in Dearborn, where he grew up. He used to be a fitness trainer and instructor.
