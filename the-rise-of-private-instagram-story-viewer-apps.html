The Rise of Private Instagram Story Viewer Apps: Are They Safe and Legal?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, May 19, 2024

It is hard to argue that those who control the information control the world. Even if you analyze modern wars, 70% of battles occur on the front line, and 30% on the internet: Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, etc. Moreover, social networks gather information about their users, which is later used for advertising, social research, and special services.

You may ask how it is connected to Instagram viewer apps. Well, it is all obvious: people do not want to register on Insta and provide Mark Zuckerberg with their personal data and consent for whatever is written in the terms, but they still need access to the information posted there. Or someone simply wants to track an Instagram anonymously for their own reasons. Whatever.

Types of Insta Viewers

This software may be divided into two types in two different categories. In terms of the service provided, there can be private Instagram viewer app and ordinary ones. As for availability, there are web-based viewers and installable mobile trackers.

What Is a Private Viewer and How Does It Differ from an Ordinary One?

Some people set their accounts to “private” mode, meaning only followers may see the content they post. However, ordinary IG viewer apps work mostly with public accounts. The idea is the following: when you watch one’s story from your personal account, the poster will see your name on the list of viewers. And if you use an anonymous viewer, there will be no notification. However, if the account is private – you won’t be able to see anything.

Private Instagram viewers, on the other hand, do all the same, but for the accounts that are locked until you follow them. 

What Is the Difference Between a Web-Based Viewer and an Installable One?

There are several cornerstones which are different for these two types of apps:

  • price;
  • features;
  • availability;
  • technical requirements.

Installable phone trackers are usually more expensive, as they offer a wider spectrum of features: SMS tracking, location tracking, access to DM messages, etc. Moreover, they are to be installed on the target device; as a result – you can operate one’s Instagram page as if it is your own. Remember that such trackers cannot be installed on the old OS versions and won’t operate properly on “ancient” devices.

Web-based trackers only require you to have the link or know the name of the target profile. You paste it in the search bar on the tracker website and can view stories, posts, and reels from the target page. However, you do not get full control of it.

The Question of Legality

The legal field is always tricky; to get the best understanding, you should consult a lawyer. However, there are two main points that may help you get a general understanding of the legality questions.

  • Always check the laws of the country where you reside at the moment. It may be that somewhere, it is clearly stated that Instagram viewer apps are illegal in the country’s jurisdiction, and one should never use them, otherwise, they might have problems with the law. In other countries, there may be nothing stated about it or some other local rules. Always check with the local law.
  • IG viewers always guarantee anonymity to their users, or there would have been no sense in them at all. Thus, you should not worry that your name appears in the log or notifications. You are hidden, you are stealthy, you are a ghost. And there are no questions to ghosts.

    • The Question of Safety

    Many people are very cautious about both public and private Instagram viewer apps as they can “compromise my personal data”. However, think logically: these applications are created to keep your data safe and hidden. How can they compromise it? 

    Of course, there are viruses and malware disguised as IG viewers on the internet, so before using any of them, check the website, read reviews, and look through the terms and FAQs. If it is malware, you will find many signs of this if you check carefully.

    Conclusion

    It is safe to say that IG viewers are usually safe and legal to use unless you come across disguised hacking software. The best thing you can do is choose the checked ones with proven effectiveness and many reviews. It even does not matter if the reviews are good or bad (well, if they are ALL bad, it is another story). If a product has only good reviews, it should be your first worrying sign.

    Generally, IG viewers are legal, but you should always double-check with your local laws to be sure there are no important nuances. It is safe to use them since such viewers guarantee anonymity to their users. The main advantage they give you is the ability to surf Instagram and check private and public accounts without registration and not logging into your account.
