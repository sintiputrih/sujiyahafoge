Karlie Kloss Net Worth 2024

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, February 13, 2024

Introduction

Known for her striking beauty and towering height, Karlie Kloss is a renowned American supermodel, entrepreneur, and philanthropist. She has graced the covers of numerous fashion magazines and walked the runway for some of the world’s most prestigious fashion houses. Beyond her modeling career, Kloss is also a tech enthusiast and has launched her own coding camp, Kode With Klossy, to empower young women in the tech industry. As of 2024, her diverse portfolio of ventures has significantly contributed to her net worth.

Modeling Career

At the tender age of 14, Karlie Kloss was discovered at a charity fashion show in her hometown of St. Louis. Her unique beauty and towering height of 6’2″ quickly caught the attention of modeling scouts. She signed with Elite Model Management in 2007 and her career took off from there.

Runway Success

Kloss has walked the runway for a plethora of high-end fashion brands, including Christian Dior, Versace, and Marc Jacobs. Her runway success has significantly contributed to her net worth.

Magazine Covers and Endorsements

Throughout her career, Kloss has graced the cover of Vogue magazine 40 times. She has also been the face of numerous advertising campaigns for brands like Adidas, Estée Lauder, and Swarovski, which have all added to her wealth.

Entrepreneurship

Beyond modeling, Kloss has also ventured into entrepreneurship. She co-founded a line of vegan and gluten-free cookies called Karlie’s Kookies, with proceeds going to charity. This venture has not only increased her net worth but also showcased her philanthropic spirit.

Kode With Klossy

In 2015, Kloss launched Kode With Klossy, a coding camp for girls aged 13-18. The initiative aims to close the gender gap in the tech industry. While it’s a non-profit, the initiative has boosted Kloss’s profile and indirectly contributed to her net worth.

Philanthropy

Kloss is also known for her philanthropic efforts. She has used her platform to raise awareness and funds for various causes, including children’s health and education, disaster relief, and the fight against hunger.

Feeding America

Kloss is a member of the board of directors for Feeding America, a non-profit organization that feeds more than 46 million people through food pantries, soup kitchens, shelters, and other community-based agencies.

Charitable Collaborations

She has also collaborated with several brands to create products where proceeds go to charity. For instance, she partnered with Warby Parker to create the Karlie Kloss x Warby Parker eyewear line, with proceeds going to Edible Schoolyard NYC.

Personal Investments

Kloss has also made personal investments that have contributed to her net worth. She is a venture partner at 1843 Capital, an early-stage venture capital firm that invests in women-led businesses.

Real Estate

She owns a $10 million home in New York City, which she shares with her husband, Joshua Kushner. The value of this property significantly adds to her net worth.

Conclusion

As of 2024, Karlie Kloss’s net worth is estimated to be around $40 million. Her wealth comes from a diverse range of sources, including her successful modeling career, entrepreneurial ventures, philanthropic efforts, and personal investments. Despite her success, Kloss remains committed to using her platform to make a positive impact, particularly in empowering young women in the tech industry. Her story is a testament to the power of hard work, passion, and the courage to venture beyond one’s comfort zone.
