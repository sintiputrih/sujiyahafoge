Chrisean Rock Says She's Considered Removing Blueface Tattoos But Doesn't Believe She Ever Will

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, August 3, 2024

No matter how heinous he is to her, Chrisean Rock will always manage to find love in her heart for Blueface. The 23-year-old has several tattoos dedicated to the "Thotiana" rapper. As of earlier this year, she has at least seven, many of them quite large and in painful places. Interestingly, Blue refuses to get any permanent tributes to her on his body. While their current relationship status remains unclear to the public, Rock is quickly approaching the due date of their first baby together.

Over the past six months, we've followed the Cr*zy In Love costars on a wild rollercoaster that's seen them separate and reunite more times than we can count. Just when Rock seems to be ready to leave Blueface and his toxicity behind, cameras catch them happily clubbing together, only for things to blow up again within a matter of days. Last week the Baltimore native confirmed that a paternity test states that the 26-year-old is the father and that she's considering an at-home birth. During a more recent Live session, Chrisean addressed questions about her ink dedicated to her future co-parent.
