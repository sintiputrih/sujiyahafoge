Who Are Rama and Felicitas From Sex Love And Goop Netflix? Everything To Know

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, January 17, 2024

If you wonder who Rama and Felicitas are from Sex, Love, And Goop, they are one of the five couples on the show who were very close to divorce because of their lost intimacy.

Sex, Love, And Goop is a reality show that helps couples gain their lost sexual intimacy and allows them to indulge in a pleasurable and intimate relationship.

Rama and Felicitas were facing problems in their sexual life after being busy in their career and children, although it was a kind of love at first sight when they saw each other in their college days.

They met each other at a party at Vassar College, where Felicitas was a student and Rama was a History and economics undergrad. They knew each other in September and were married by May.

Being no different from other ordinary couples, they lost their spark of marriage, which brought them to the show.

Rama And Felicitas Age

Rama Jaima was born in June on the exact day of international father’s day, but his birth year is not known yet. Similarly, Felicitas’s birth date is unrevealed.

Yet we can guess them to be in their thirties. Since Rama migrated from the United States for further education in 2006, he must be in his mid-thirties.

Felicitas being relatively younger than him, might be in her early thirties.

Rama And Felicitas Instagram Bio

Rama keeps his Instagram account private with 416 followers, 2,525 followings, and 208 posts. Similarly, Felicitas has 796 followers, 2,099 followings, and 2,087 posts.

She is an entrepreneur at @gardenstudiosandiego, which is her plant-based healthier Airbnb in Sandiego. She enlists California and Germany in her bio.

On the other hand, she is found posting lots of pictures of her daughter and son. Recently, they were pictured spending quality time with their children on the 12th of October.

Are Rama And Felicitas Still Together?

Rama and Felicitas are still together, and the pictures on Felicitas’s Instagram are its proof. Moreover, they admitted that their bonding was improving after the show.

Even though they are on the journey of learning to heal and overcoming hardships, it’s getting better for sure. It was years since she posted her husband’s picture, but now they are seen close on photos as well.

This is unquestionably a positive indicator for fans to speculate on how the show has benefited their marriage.
