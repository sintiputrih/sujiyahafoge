Bruce Willis and Emma Heming Have Been Married for 10 Years - Here's Their Love Story

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 24, 2024

Bruce Willis spent about a decade alone after his first marriage failed, but his second marriage to Emma Heming is a story of love, acceptance, and an unbreakable bond.

In March, Bruch Willis renewed his vows with his wife Emma Heming on their 10th anniversary. The couple got married in 2009 after dating for about a year. Speaking with US Weekly, Heming, who is a model and an actress, opened up about what makes their relationship work.

Bruce Willis at the game between the Milwaukee Brewers and Philadelphia Phillies in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.| Photo: Getty Images.

THEY BUILT A GREAT FOUNDATION

The actress said about her 10-year marriage,

"I think we've always had a lot of respect for each other, which has really helped build a great foundation."

"He makes me laugh; he's funny, and he's smart, and nuts, and always keeps me on my toes, so it's fun."

Bruce Willis and Emma Heming at the "Glass" NY Premiere in New York City.| Photo: Getty Images.

Heming revealed that things equally work because her children with Willis, Mabel, and Evelyn, get along with their sisters — Rumer, Scout, and Tallulah — from their father's first marriage to Demi Moore. She said,

"When the older ones are around, the little ones want nothing to do with Bruce or me."

Bruce Willis and wife Emma Heming Willis at the "Motherless Brooklyn" Arrivals during the 57th New York Film Festival in New York City.| Photo: Getty Images.

THEY HAVE A BLENDED FAMILY

The actress also has a great relationship with Moore, whom she has a lot of respect for, and welcomed into her family. Between them, they have a perfectly blended family, and it helps that Willis loves being around Heming.

"I hear so many people in relationships say, I just need a couple of days away, or even an hour away. But I don't ever want to be away from Emma at all. It's the most singular relationship I've ever had in my life."

Bruce Willis at the "Motherless Brooklyn" premiere during the 57th New York Film Festival in New York City.| Photo: Getty Images.

WILLIS IS CONTENT

The "Die Hard" actor and Heming met at a gym and got introduced by a mutual friend, who also served as their fitness trainer. Before meeting the actress, Willis, whose marriage to Moore ended in 2000, dated a few Hollywood celebs like Brooke Burns and Karen McDougal.

Bruce Willis at the 17th Annual A Great Night In Harlem in New York City.| Photo: Getty Images.

With Heming, the "Red" actor is content and happy with his life. Outside their marriage, the mom-of-two and Willis have collaborated on some projects, and starred together in the movie, "Perfect Strangers." The New York resident has equally modelled for brands like Chanel, Ralph Lauren, and Valentino.

The actress and the actor are committed to their family and plan to move west to be closer to their daughters' siblings.
