Sara Just is the executive producer of PBS NewsHour. She is also the Senior Vice President and WETA at PBS NewsHour and leads the editorial and production team for the nightly broadcast and its online products.

She joined the PBS team in September 2014. It has been 7 years and is continuously still working there.

Before working at PBS, Sara spent 25 years at ABC News. Just was recently the Washington Deputy Bureau Chief and senior producer for Good Morning America.
