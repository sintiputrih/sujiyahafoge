Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, April 19, 2024

How do I cancel my GoDaddy domain?

Delete my domain
  • Log in to your GoDaddy Domain Control Center. (Need help logging in?
  • Check the box next to the domain(s) you’ll be deleting. Or, click the check mark icon and Select All.
  • Select More then Delete.
  • Confirm the domains being deleted and check the box next to Yes, I consent to delete the selected domains..
  • Select Continue.

    • How do I cancel domain renewal on GoDaddy?

    Go to your GoDaddy Renewals & Billing page. Check the box to select the product or domain you want to turn off auto-renew. Select Cancel Renewal. A new window will appear.

    How do I cancel my domain subscription?

    Click Manage Domains. Next to your domain, click View Details for additional information. Click Cancel your billing subscription. In the Cancel your domain billing subscription panel, review the consequences of canceling your domain billing subscription.

    Can you refund domain GoDaddy?

    Refundable during the first 48 hours if there are no bids. If a Domain Backorder credit has been applied to an auction, then it is non-refundable (if not yet applied to an auction, eligible for a refund within 30 days of the date of the transaction).

    Can I get a refund on a domain name?

    Domain names: Most domain name purchases can be refunded within the first five (5) days of purchase, provided that we remove the domain from your possession and return it to the registry (which means it will then be available for purchase). Domain name renewals are never refundable.

    How do I get a refund from GoDaddy?

    Requesting a Refund on GoDaddy

    After you cancel a product or service you no longer need, if you qualify for a refund, you will need to contact GoDaddy customer support. Refund requests are possible only through: Live chat—Go to GoDaddy Contact page and click on the Chat Now box, if available.

    How do I dispute a charge on GoDaddy?

    To file a dispute, send an email to AuctionDisputes@godaddy.com that includes the following details:
  • Your full name and contact information.
  • Your account number.
  • The domain name you won.
  • The order number for the transaction.
  • Your detailed account of the dispute.

    • Can I cancel my GoDaddy account?

    Cancel Products: If you are no longer using a product, you can cancel it. Please contact customer support to see if you are eligible for a refund. If you have cancelled all of your products, and no longer want an account, you can close your GoDaddy account.

    What happens if my domain expires GoDaddy?

    Godaddy will hold the domain after is expires for a period of several months. If your renew domain more than 19 days after expiry, then there is a redemption fee added. Even after the 42 days, the domain may not go back onto the market.

    What happens if you don’t renew your domain name?

    If you do not renew the domain name during the registry grace period, it will be placed on a pending status to be deleted. If the original owner or the registrar doesn’t buy the domain, it will be deleted and released for general registration.

    What happens if you forget to renew your domain?

    Once the grace period passes, things can really get expensive. Your domain registrar will now file a cancellation request with ICANN – essentially telling them that the previous domain registration is now null and void. Your domain then enters the dreaded Redemption Period.

    How long do you have to renew a domain after it expires?

    You‘ll have 30 days (for most domains*) after your registration expires to renew your domain at the standard rate. After 30 days*, it’ll still be possible to renew, but you‘ll have to pay an additional fee — typically $100 (for a .com domain).

    How do I renew an expired domain name?

    Renewing expired domain names

  • Under Expired Domains, click Renew next to the domain name you want.

  • Follow the instructions to complete the order.
