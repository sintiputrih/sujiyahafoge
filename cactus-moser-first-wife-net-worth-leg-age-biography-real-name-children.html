Cactus Moser First Wife, Net Worth, Leg, Age, Biography, Real Name, Children

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, September 5, 2024

Cactus Moser is a drummer, vocalist, writer, producer, multi instrumentalist, three-time Modern Drummer readers poll winner, Four time CMA winner and 3 time Grammy Award nominee.

Most notably, Cactus Moser has been a member of the highly successful, Grammy Nominated band Highway 101 for over 20 years.

Cactus Moser age

Cactus Moser was born on May 3, 1957.

Cactus Moser real name

Cactus Moser’s real name is Michael Scott Moser.

Cactus Moser first wife

Wynonna Judd is the first wife of Cactus Moser.

Wynonna Judd and Cactus Moser got engaged on December 24, 2011 and eventually got married on June 10, 2012, at her home in Leiper’s Fork, Tennessee.

Cactus Moser children

Cactus Moser has three grown children: Sunshine, 33, Cahl, 23, and Wyatt, 18 (ages of the children are as of 2012).

Cactus Moser leg

On August 18, 2012, Cactus Moser was severely injured in a motorcycle crash in South Dakota.

He was on U.S. Route 16 in the Black Hills when he crossed the center line and hit a car, which resulted in his left leg having to be amputated above the knee.
