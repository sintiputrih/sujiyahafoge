Is Travis Kelce taller than George Kittle?

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, September 30, 2024

When comparing the physical attributes of Travis Kelce and George Kittle, there are some intriguing differences and similarities to consider.

Physical Attributes

  • Height and Speed
    • Both Kelce and Kittle are known for their impressive size and speed, which are crucial assets for tight ends in the NFL. Kittle stands at six foot four inches tall, while Kelce is slightly taller at six foot five inches.

    • Statistical Achievements

  • Receiving Yards and Receptions

    • In terms of their 2023 season statistics, Kittle recorded 1,020 receiving yards with 65 receptions, while Kelce amassed 984 receiving yards with 93 receptions.

    • Career-wise, Kittle has accumulated 6,274 receiving yards in 98 games, with 460 receptions and an average of 13.6 yards per catch. On the other hand, Kelce has 11,328 receiving yards in 159 games, with 907 receptions and an average of 12.5 yards per reception.

  • Yards After Catch (YAC)

    • Kittle consistently excels in YAC, having the most YAC count out of any WR/TE in the last two years. Kelce, while also impressive, has the second most YAC, trailing Kittle by 517 yards. Kittle's YAC per reception (7.3) is also higher than Kelce's (4.8).

    • On-Field Play

  • Playing Style

    • Kittle's playing style has been described as a combination of exceptional blocking ability, speed, toughness, and safe hands, making him a versatile and impactful player on the field.

    • Kelce, known for his rapport with Mahomes, has demonstrated high-level play, serving as Mahomes' safety valve and making crucial catches in tight spots.

  • Career Comparison

    • Kittle's career averages 64.0 yards per game receiving and 4.7 receptions per game, while Kelce averages 71.2 yards per game receiving and 5.7 receptions per game.
  • Overall Evaluation
    • While Kelce is considered a terrific player and has an exceptional partnership with Mahomes, Kittle's well-rounded skill set, particularly in blocking and YAC, gives him a slight edge in the tight end position.

    • while both Travis Kelce and George Kittle are exceptional tight ends with similar physical attributes, statistical achievements, and on-field prowess, Kittle's proficiency in YAC and blocking, combined with his career statistics, make him a formidable force in the NFL. Their differences in playing style and statistical performances contribute to the ongoing debate of who is the better tight end.
