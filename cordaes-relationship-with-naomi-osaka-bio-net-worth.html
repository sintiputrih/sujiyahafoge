Cordaes Relationship with Naomi Osaka, Bio, Net Worth, and Other Details

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, June 27, 2024

Cordae, the Grammy-nominated American rapper, said goodbye to his affiliation with his crew, YBN, in 2020. However, Cordae has stated numerous times that he still considers Jay and Nahmir to be his real-life brothers. The rapper has recently made headlines for his music and his relationship with No. 1 ranked tennis star, girlfriend, Naomi Osaka.

Cordae- Birth, Age, Ethnicity, Siblings

YBN Cordae (real name: Cordae Dunston) was born in 1997 in Raleigh, North Carolina. Every year on August 26th, the Atlantic Records artist celebrates his birthday. He was the eldest child in his family, with four siblings born after him. Cordae, who is of African-American descent, was raised by his single mother alongside his siblings.

What is the Net Worth of Cordae? Salary, Earnings

The rapper in his twenties earns a good living from his musical works. His YouTube earnings are estimated to be more than $10,000 per year. Cordae currently has a net worth of more than $500,000k.

Cordae- Relationship, Married Life

Cordae’s love life with Naomi Osaka has been on fire, as the couple is frequently seen together, and they have been married for two years.

The Grammy nominee and tennis star’s apparent relationship was revealed earlier this year when the tennis star shared a video of the two hanging out and flirting with each other.

Cordae has been extremely supportive of the multiple Grand Slam champion. Amid the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020, Osaka was forced to quarantine alone ahead of the 2020 U.S. Open. Cordae flew out to be there for her, which his girlfriend, Osaka, described as “the most romantic thing.”

The couple frequently shares their love with their fans on social media. Their fans all over the world are eager to see what the future holds for them.

Gay Opinions

Cordae has frequently expressed his views on the gay community, even though he has never come out as gay. For example, in 2013, he began a tweet questioning his sexuality after listening to R. Kelly.

The rapper, formerly known as Entendre, allegedly meant the statement as a joke, but many assumed he was gay. Later that month, in January 2015, he sent out another tweet, this time to his younger brother. Cordae stated that his 11-year-old brother became a gay man because he still believed in Santa Claus and was afraid of the dark.

Many people chastised him, claiming that being gay does not imply being stupid. He was also shamed for his disparaging remarks about gay people.

Cordae- Professional Career

Scottie Pippen was obsessed with music as a child and dreamed of pursuing a career as a musician. He began mixing various hit singles and released his mixtape Anxiety in 2014, which brought him initial fame.

With the positive feedback, he decided to focus more on his music and released two more mixtapes, I’m So Anxious and I’m So Anonymous, in 2016 and 2017.

Cordae joined the Young Boss N*ggaz (YBN) in 2017 after the group was founded in 2014 by YBN Almighty Jay and YBN Nahmir.
Rubbin Off The Paint, Bread Winners, Kung Fu, and other severe hip-hop hits were released by the crew as a group.

However, after six years of collaboration, the crewmates’ internal feud forced the team to disband.
After the crew disbanded, YBN Cordae dropped the suffix from his name.
