Nina Dobrev &amp; Julianne Hough Team Up to Launch Wine Company!

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, June 4, 2024

Nina Dobrev and Julianne Hough have been best friends for years and now they are working together on a brand new business idea!

The actresses have teamed up to launch Fresh Vine Wine, an exclusive collection of premium low-carb, low-calorie, gluten-free, Keto, and vegan-friendly wines available nationwide.

The wines are produced in Napa, California and the collection features a California Cabernet Sauvignon, Pinot Noir, Chardonnay, California Rosé, and a Limited Reserve Napa Cabernet.

Nina said in a statement, “Julianne and I were inspired to create Fresh Vine Wines after noticing the growing number of consumers making healthier choices surrounding wellness and longevity while also searching for a wine that compliments their lifestyle choices instead of undermining them. We have similar work schedules and at the end of the day, we love unwinding with a glass of wine. We always had this dream to venture into the wine industry and create a product that reflected who we are. After meeting with the Fresh Vine Wine team, we immediately knew that this was an incredible opportunity to partner and together we could try to create something that evoked a guilt-free experience.”

Julianne added, “The very foundation of my friendship with Nina is built upon experiencing and sharing life together, always with a glass of wine in hand. We both lead very active and physical lifestyles, with a holistic approach, so we wanted to make a product that not only complimented our lives but was also still of premium quality and impeccable taste. If we learned anything from the experience of living through the year 2020, it was that connection and celebrating the small things in life are so incredibly important to the emotional, mental and physical well-being of humankind. Because we were all forced to stay apart, finding alternative ways to connect was imperative. It was incredible to see people finding other ways to bond – including many virtual wine nights! As we look toward brighter days where we all can be together safely in person, Fresh Vine Wine will be a great way to toast to each other with a high quality, delicious product.”

The California Cabernet Sauvignon ($21.89), Pinot Noir ($18.99), and Chardonnay ($17.99) are available today for purchase on FreshVineWine.com. Both the California Rosé ($14.89) and Limited Reserve Napa Cabernet ($80) are available for pre-sale nationwide on FreshVineWine.com, with the California Rosé debuting this Spring/Summer and the Limited Reserve Napa Cabernet in the Fall.
