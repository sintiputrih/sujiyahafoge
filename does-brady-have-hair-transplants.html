Does Brady have hair transplants?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, January 21, 2024

Thomas Edward Patrick Brady Jr. is an accomplished American football quarterback who plies his trade for the National Football League’s Tampa Bay Buccaneers.

He spent the first twenty seasons of his career with the New England Patriots, where he was a key player the whole time.

Does Brady have hair transplants?

Definitely, per a report from the National Enquirer, the New England Patriots quarterback underwent a hair transplant at the request of his supermodel wife Gisele Bundchen.

The world’s highest-paid supermodel became concerned that Brady was losing his charms and referred him to a professional for immediate treatment

“Gisele wants to keep that perfect-couple image, the attractive quarterback with a head full of hair and the lovely model with her own long flowing locks,” according to the Enquirer.

Who did Tom Brady’s hair transplants?

Dr. Robert Leonard of Leonard Hair Transplant Association was the same hairstylist who repaired former Boston Bruin Lyndon Byers’ hair.

He was reported to help Tom Brady look much younger and attractive again. This procedure cost millions of dollars and the Brady’s were able to afford it.
