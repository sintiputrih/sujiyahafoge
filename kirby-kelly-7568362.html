Kirby Kelly - InStyle

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, March 31, 2024

Title: Freelance Writer

Education: The Ohio State University

Location: Denver, CO

Kirby Kelly is a copywriter with an agency background. She's worked with both lifestyle brands and major global corporations, but her favorite product categories are activewear, beauty, and wellness brands.

Experience

I am a competitive road cyclist, so I know a thing or two about bikes (and what products are needed to train 15+ hours a week). I was also a personal trainer once upon a time and still love hitting the gym. The amount of time I spend working out definitely dictates what I wear—and I have recommendations upon recommendations!

Education

Bachelor's degree in English Literature from The Ohio State University.

About InStyle

InStyle is an award-winning destination for fashion, beauty, and pop-culture, with an all-are-welcome ethos that swings open the proverbial velvet ropes. Whether you’re looking for a wardrobe refresh, haircut inspiration, or a story that’ll make sparkling cocktail-party conversation, our expert-connected coverage is where to start.
