Holly Willoughby deletes bathtime photo after fans spot presenter's reflection

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, April 4, 2024

Holly Willoughby received a word of warning from well-meaning fans on Sunday after her latest social media post threatened to reveal more than she intended. 

The This Morning co-host, an active Instagram user, gave followers an insight into her day off by sharing a photo of her gleaming bath taps as she prepared for a soak in the family tub. 

But fans were quick to point out a potential gaffe after spotting her partial reflection in the polished faucets as she leaned in to take the photo, which has since been deleted.
