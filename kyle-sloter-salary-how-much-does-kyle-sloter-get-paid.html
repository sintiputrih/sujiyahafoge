Who Is Kyle Sloter?

Kyle Sloter is an American football quarterback born on February 7, 1994.

Kyle played college football at Southern Miss and then Northern Colorado. The Denver Broncos had signed him in 2017 as an undrafted free agent.

He has played in different teams including the Minnesota Vikings, Arizona Cardinals, Detroit Lions, Chicago Bears, and Las Vegas Raiders.
