Wilmer Valderrama News - Us Weekly

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, May 24, 2024

Name:Wilmer Valderrama

Hometown:Miami, FL

Relationship:Single

Birthday:January 30, 1980

About

The Colombian and Venezuelan actor was born on January 30, 1980 in Miami, Fla., but moved to Venezuela when he was three years old.

Known for his hilarious act as exchange student Fez on That ’70s Show, Wilmer Valderamma created and executive produced his own MTV show, Yo Momma, and he took on small roles in two films released in the fall of 2006, Fast Food Nation and Unaccompanied Minors.

Also making headlines due to his penchant for young Hollywood starlets like Lindsay Lohan, Jennifer Love Hewitt and Ashlee Simpson, Valderrama claimed he and Mandy Moore were each other’s “first loves.”
