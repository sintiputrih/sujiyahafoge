19 Famous People and Celebrities Who are Sagittarius

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, September 17, 2024

Sagittarius is the sign of the adventurer, the philosopher, and the free spirit. Many celebrities have this astrological sign and embody the traits of the sign in their personal and professional lives.

Here are some famous people born under the Sagittarius sign:

Brad Pitt (December 18)

Brad Pitt is a Sagittarius who embodies the sign’s love for travel and adventure. He has used his talents to become one of the most successful actors of his generation, and he has become a household name through his work in Hollywood.

Britney Spears (December 2)

Britney Spears is a Sagittarius who is known for her rebellious nature. She has had many career highs and lows, but she has remained happy and healthy with a cute family and a residency in Vegas.

DJ Khaled (November 26)

DJ Khaled is a Sagittarius who is known for his expensive lifestyle and fabulous travels. He has used his talents to become a successful musician and has become a household name through his music and social media presence.

Hailey Bieber (November 22)

Hailey Bieber is a Sagittarius who is impulsive and devoted. She got engaged to pop superstar Justin Bieber after only one month of dating, and she is committed to her faith.

Jamie Foxx (December 13)

Jamie Foxx is a Sagittarius who follows his sign’s tendency to be a “sponge” with an inner quest for truth and knowledge. He has used his talents to become a great actor who can get really into his characters, especially in biopics.

Jay-Z (December 4)

Jay-Z is a Sagittarius who is known for his expensive lifestyle and his many businesses. He has used his talents to become one of the most successful rap artists of all time and has become a household name through his music and business ventures.

Katie Holmes (December 18)

Katie Holmes is a Sagittarius who has an interest in philosophy and religion. She has used her talents to become a successful actress and has become a respected voice in Hollywood.

Miley Cyrus (November 23)

Miley Cyrus is a Sagittarius who embodies the sign’s creative, strong-willed, and blunt qualities. She has used her talents to become a successful musician and has become a pop culture icon through her unique fashion and old partying antics.

Nicki Minaj (December 8)

Nicki Minaj is a Sagittarius who is known for her bluntness and self-sufficiency. She has used her talents to become a figure of female empowerment and has become one of the most successful female rappers of all time.

Ozzy Osbourne (December 3)

Ozzy Osbourne is a Sagittarius who is known for his energetic performances and wild lifestyle. He has used his talents to become a successful rockstar and has become a legend in the music industry.

Raven Symone (December 10)

Raven Symone is a Sagittarius who can be argumentative and blunt, but she has turned this trait into one of her best qualities. She has used her talents to become a successful actress and has become a powerful activist, using her platform to advocate for important causes.

Rita Ora (November 26)

Rita Ora is a Sagittarius who lives a lavish lifestyle in true Sagittarius form, using her musical earnings to fund adventurous trips around the world. She has used her talents to become a successful musician and has become a fashion icon through her bold fashion choices.

Samuel L. Jackson (December 21)

Samuel L. Jackson is a Sagittarius who is known for his consistent talent and scandal-free film career. He has used his talents to become one of the most respected actors in Hollywood and has become a household name through his work in the film industry.

Scarlett Johansson (November 22)

Scarlett Johansson is a Sagittarius who lies right on the Scorpio-Sagittarius cusp with a November 22 birthday. She embodies the Scorpio moon’s influence on her sex appeal and the Sagittarius in her sun sign leads to a little bit of deep-seated rebellion. She has used her talents to become one of the most successful actresses of her generation and has become a respected voice in Hollywood.

Sia (December 18)

Sia is a Sagittarius who is private off-stage but fearless on-stage. She embodies her sign’s traits of creativity, charm, and a strong will. She has used her talents to become a successful musician and has become a pop culture icon through her unique style and powerful vocals.

Steven Spielberg (December 18)

Steven Spielberg is a Sagittarius who is the ultimate devoted artist. He has used his talents to become one of the founding pioneers of the New Hollywood era of film and has thrown everything he has into everything he creates.

Taylor Swift (December 13)

Taylor Swift is a Sagittarius who is not known for biting her tongue. She embodies her sign’s tendency to be outspoken and has used her talents to become one of the most successful musicians of her generation. Her biting lyrics never fail to stir up media controversy, and she has become a pop culture icon through her music and public persona.

Vanessa Hudgens (December 14)

Vanessa Hudgens is a Sagittarius who is independent and hates being told how to live. She embodies her sign’s non-conforming spirit and has used her talents to become a successful actress and fashion icon. Her bold fashion choices show her rebellious spirit in the most stylish way.

Ben Stiller (November 30)

Ben Stiller is a Sagittarius who is smart and a go-getter. He embodies his sign’s energetic approach to life and has used his talents to become a successful actor and filmmaker. He has maintained a prolific movie career for two decades straight while staying a family man and maintaining a private life.

Popular Celebrities and Famous People that are Sagittarians

In conclusion, Sagittarians are known for their adventurous spirit, philosophical nature, and free-spirited approach to life. These celebrities have used their talents to become successful in their respective fields, embodying the traits of the Sagittarius sign along the way.

From actors to musicians, fashion icons, and activists, Sagittarians have left their mark on the world and continue to inspire others with their unique perspectives and talents.
