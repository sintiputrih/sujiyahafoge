Matty Healy Biography

Matty Healy Education

Matthew was enrolled at Wilmslow High School where he began jamming with his friends, Ross MacDonald, Adam Hann and George Daniel in 2004. They group began by playing punk – rock and pop covers before they started writing their own music at the age of about 15. He did not do too well in school and finished with three GCSEs. Matty Healy Career

Healy began his career with playing drums for ‘The 1975’. He later took on as the role of lead singer and guitarist after their lead singer left the band to form his own group. His band members included Adam Hann as the lead guitarist, Ross MacDonald on the bass and George Daniel playing the drums.

The group produced their first EP, ‘Facedown’, that was released in 2012 and got to be aired on national radio, with the lead single, ‘The City’, it became a hit on BBC Radio 1. Them rising to fame came as a shock to them as they were making music more for fun than as a profession.
