Holly Rice: Who Is Vince Gilligans Wife?

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, April 4, 2024

Holly Rice is the beautiful wife of Breaking Bad and Better Call Saul producer, Vince Gilligan.

Rice has made a cameo role in one of her husband’s productions.

Rice and Vince dated for three decades before they got married.

They began dating in 1990, and Rice was a teacher at a Chesterfield school at the time.

Who Is Vince Gilligan?

Vincent Gilligan is an American screenwriter, producer and director, born on February 10, 1967.

Vince is well known for producing AMC’s Breaking Bad (2008–2013) and its spin-off prequel series Better Call Saul (2015–2022).

He also wrote and produced The X-Files (1993–2002; 2016–2018) as well a its sequel The Lone Gunmen (2001). He also co-wrote and produced the movie Hancock.

Vince Gilligan Awards

Vince has won several awards including four Primetime Emmy Awards, six Writers Guild of America Awards, two Critics’ Choice Television Awards, two Producers Guild of America Awards, a Directors Guild of America Award, and a BAFTA Television Award.
