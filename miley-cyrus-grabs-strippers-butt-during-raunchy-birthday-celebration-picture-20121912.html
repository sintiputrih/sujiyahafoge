Miley Cyrus Grabs Stripper's Butt During Raunchy Birthday Celebration: Picture

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, April 22, 2024

Well, she’s just being Miley.

New photos from the Miley Cyrus‘ birthday celebration last month may have more than a few tongues clucking following hot on the heels of her most recent raunchy antics at the VH1 Divas Concert Sunday Dec. 16.

In the images, the Hannah Montana star, who turned 20 on Nov. 23, can be seen grabbing a stripper’s bare bottom and making faces at the camera during the wild Hollywood house party.

The stripper, whose back is to the camera, has on little more than a bra top and barely-there hot pink G-string in the picture. Cyrus, covered up in an all-black ensemble, grits her face in the jokey photo.

To be fair, though, Cyrus did tweet out a warning to her friends and fans prior to her birthday bash. 

“If I don’t get at least one big booty hoe my friends are officially not my friends anymore,” she tweeted, a clear nod to rapper 2 Chainz’s “Birthday Song.”

The former Disney star has come under fire recently for her partying ways and bold behavior, but Cyrus has thus far dismissed critics as “idiots.”

Earlier last week, E! News’ Ken Baker posted a graphic of Cyrus and fellow young singer Taylor Swift and pointed out that Cyrus was being called “a slut or a bitch every day” though she is monogamously engaged to Liam Hemsworth, while Swift is viewed as “sweet, elegant and inspirational” though she’s dated “more than 13 guys for the past three years.”
