Becky G is a talented Mexican and American personality. Becky G is a singer, actress, and spokesmodel who started getting popular in 2011 after she started uploading her cover videos of famous songs online.

Early Life

The beautiful singer was born on 2nd March 1997, in Inglewood, California, U. S. The birth name of Becky G is Rebbeca Marie Gomez. Likewise, Becky G is a Mexican-American native and is of Hispanic ethnicity. Also, she follows Christianity.

As of 2024, the singer must be twenty-six years old and her sun sign is Pisces. The name of her parents is Alejandra “Alex” Gomez and Francisco “Frank” Gomez. They first got to meet each other at Santa Monica High School where they became high school sweethearts.

Moreover, she also has three siblings and their names are Rankie, Alex, and Stephanie. Her grandparents migrated from Jalisco and Mexico to the United States. Only after then, did her parents, and she was born.

Unfortunately, her family lost their home because of some financial problems. Then, her family went to live in a mini garage. She was just 9 years old when she decided to begin pursuing a career in the entertainment industry to help her family survive.

Then, she started looking for child talent agencies, luckily found them, and even started getting jobs in commercials, voiceovers, and modeling as well. She has even shared some information about her hard work with anxiety.

She has also thanked her family for supporting and helping her to face all those problems. At first, the singer went to a public school and then was homeschooled because of bullying.

She even said that once several girls jumped over her when she was in the restroom and were always targeted because of her work in the entertainment industry.

Some of her Spanish singles are “Sola,” “Todo Cambio,” “Sin Pijama” (feat Natti Natasha), and “Mayores”. “Mayores”‘s videos have a catchy song that represents the rapper Bad Bunny.

She has mentioned that videos for her Spanish-language songs should promote the music to get into English-speaking audiences since they’ll be able to understand the song from those “mini-movies”.

She has agreed that her Spanish was not good when she began making Spanish-language music. But she was still very proud and happy to keep the contact with her heritage. She was present with the guest appearance as Valentina on the television show “Empire” in 2015.

She then made her huge-screen appearance as Trini, the Yellow Ranger, in 2017’s movie version of the 1990s television show “Power Rangers”. Similarly, she also did the voice acting of a character in Gnome Alone in the year 2017.

In 2018, she was present in the family adventure movie “A.X.L.”
