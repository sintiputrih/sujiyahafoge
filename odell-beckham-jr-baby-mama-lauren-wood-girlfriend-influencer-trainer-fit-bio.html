Who Is Odell Beckham Jr.'s Baby Mama Lauren Wood?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, March 20, 2024

Let's be honest here, Odell Beckham Jr. doesn't have the greatest track record. When it comes to his NFL career, drama seems to follow him and the same can be said for his personal life. Former baby mama Celina Powell had some harsh things to say in relation to her time with Odell, making some harsh claims.

We assume his new partner, Lauren Wood is putting the past aside, as the two recently revealed that they're set on becoming parents.

Fans know very little about Lauren Wood, however, as we'll reveal, she is no stranger to the spotlight. We'll take a look at her past on television and some of the shows she appeared on, along with what she's doing nowadays.

Wood has a huge following via social media, with a near 2 million followers. Let's find out why so many fans clicked the follow button.

Lauren Wood Has A Reality TV Background

Sure, most fans are accustomed to seeing Odell Beckham Jr. on TV given his prominent NFL career, however, partner Lauren Wood knows a thing or two about the bright lights as well. She appeared on reality TV back in 2015, appearing on the MTV series 'Wild 'N Out'.

Not only did she take part in the sixth and seventh season but according to Browns Nation, she was voted as a fan favorite in 2015. That wasn't her only accolades on TV, she also appeared in Dwayne Johnson's series, 'Ballers'.

In terms of age, Wood and Beckham Jr. aren't very apart. The NFL star was born in 1992 while his girlfriend Lauren Wood was born a year later in '93.

These days, she's keeping busy with a massive following on social media, in large part thanks to her positivity and fitness posts. She has a following most of us could only dream of, with nearly two million followers at this time.

Wood Is An Influencer And Fitness Trainer

She has 1.7 million followers via Instagram. Take a quick scroll down Lauren's page and it becomes obvious quickly, she's an influencer and great fitness trainer, posting anywhere from fitness content, such as the proper way to do squats, to inspirational photos with some catchy captions.

One of her recent posts discussed the importance of self-awareness. It is important to not only look good but feel good as well.

''Humanity is teaching us pain can be easily masked by money, which greatly fuels this self-centered culture we live in. More people are driven by greed, vanity, jealousy. It’s attributing to so many of us losing basic human instincts that are born in light & love, such as compassion, empathy & basic levels of respect."

"We’re all guilty of it. There’s a lack of stepping outside ourselves & our ability to connect with one another just off the simple fact that we are all humans navigating life and sometimes find ourselves in isolation & discontent. It’s time to raise our self-awareness and take accountability for ourselves and those around us."

Some impactful words from the influencer, her IG account is filled with motivational quotes, along with a plethora of other topics, including Q&A rituals.

According to the former 'Wild 'N Out' star, keeping consistent is a major part of her success on social media.

These days, her name seems to be trending a little bit more. The biggest reason is due to the fact that she's expecting a child alongside Odell Beckham Jr.

Wood And Odell Beckham Jr. Are Starting A Family

Unlike other NFL stars, Beckham Jr. decided to keep his relationship alongside Wood quiet. The two have been together for quite some time, dating back to a couple of years ago.

According to Odell's words on IG, the two have been through quite a bit together, yet they still manage to create a strong bond.

"Thru thick and thin every day with u has been a blessing. I know that God has placed you in my life for a special reason, and excited about that adventure to find out why," Beckham Jr. wrote alongside a series of photos of the pair.

"My favorite thing about us is us and no one or anything in this world can come between that. Happy glow day to my hipbone! I love u YUMI! Time to elevate!!," he added.

Things seem to be getting even better for the couple as they're expecting their first child. Both broke the news on social media, with a picture post showing Beckham Jr. holding Wood's pregnant belly. It'll be another great new journey for the couple.
