The British opera world is in mourning following the sudden and tragic death of Keel Watson, a beloved bass-baritone singer. With a career spanning 25 years, Watson was a favorite in the industry, captivating audiences with his powerful voice and dynamic stage presence. Tragically, he passed away due to a heart attack while preparing to launch his boat at Shelly Beach, a place he enjoyed diving. This unexpected loss has left the music community and his fans in shock and mourning. Watson’s magnificent voice, sharp wit, and generosity as a performer will be greatly missed. His contributions to the world of opera will not be forgotten, serving as a reminder to cherish and celebrate extraordinary talents. Our thoughts go out to his family and loved ones during this difficult time.
