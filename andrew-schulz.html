Stand up comedy

In September 2017, Andrew Schulz self-released his first comedy special, “4:4:1”. His special aired on YouTube. Similarly, in June 2018, he followed up with his debut comedy album, “5:5:1”.

Moreover, in the album’s first week of release, it earned number one ranking on the iTunes comedy album charts, Apple Music, Google Play, and Amazon.

Furthermore, for the week of June 23, 2018, the album was number one on the Billboard comedy album charts.
