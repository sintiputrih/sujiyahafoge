Ryan Sessegnon is regarded as one of the fastest footballers in England. He has spent almost his entire career in England having started his professional career in Fulham and currently with Tottenham Hotspur.

This article talks about Ryan Sessegnon’s biography, age, height, wife, parents, children, net worth, and all that fans need to know about him.

Ryan Sessegnon Biography

Kouassi Ryan Sessegnon is a professional English footballer who plays his football in the English Premier League.

He started his professional career in 2016-2017 when he joined Fulham in the Championship. After two years, he was able to help Fulham get to the English Premier League. He only had a season in the English Premier League with Fulham when his performance got him a move to Tottenham Hotspur. He made a total of 120 appearances and scored 25 goals for Fulham before departing.

Sessegnon joined Tottenham in 2019-2020 and has spent 3 seasons with them including a year loan move to 1899 Hoffenheim in the Bundesliga.
He has so far made a total of 49 appearances and scored 3 goals for Tottenham Hotspur.
